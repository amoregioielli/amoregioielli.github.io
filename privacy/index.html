AmOrGioielli — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AmOrGioielli ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our use of the Pinterest API to publish our own jewelry product content (images and videos) as Pins that link to our product listings.

Who we are

AmOrGioielli is a small jewelry business. We operate an online shop and publish promotional content about our own products.

  • Company: AmOrGioielli
  • Contact email: orecchinicarini@gmail.com

What this application does

Our application uses the Pinterest API solely to:

  • Publish our own product photos and videos as Pins on our own Pinterest business account.
  • Attach titles, descriptions, and links (to our own product listings) to those Pins.
  • Read and manage our own boards and Pins to organize the content we publish.

The application acts only on our own Pinterest account, using credentials we own and authorize.

Information we collect

The application does not collect, store, or process personal data from Pinterest users or any third parties.

  • We do not access, request, or store data about other Pinterest users, their accounts, their boards, or their activity.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • The only data handled is content we create and own (our product images, videos, titles, descriptions, and links), plus the OAuth access tokens needed to authenticate to our own Pinterest account. These tokens are stored securely and used only to operate our own account.

Use of the Pinterest API

Our use of the Pinterest API complies with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines and the Pinterest Terms of Service. We use Pinterest data only as permitted by those terms and only for the purposes described above.

Data retention and security

Authentication tokens are stored locally and securely, and are used only to publish and manage content on our own account. We retain this information only as long as necessary to operate the application.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at orecchinicarini@gmail.com.
